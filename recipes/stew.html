Let's make some STEW!

Ingredients

  • Two pounds of chuck roast
  • One bag of gold potatoes
  • Two large carrots
  • One yellow onion
  • 6-8 cups of beef stock
  • Two tablespoons of red wine vinegar
  • One cup of red or white wine
  • One small can of tomato paste

Seasonings

  • Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Rosemary

Directions

  1. Cut roast into 1.5 inch cubes
  2. Dice carrots and union
  3. Brown each side of the meat
  4. Remove meat and cook carrots and onion in remaining oil
  5. Once softened, add garlic and tomato paste. Cook for 1-2 more minutes.
  6. Pour in wine, deglaze pot, and reduce until half
  7. Add red wine vinegar and broth, bring to a low boil and add meat
  8. Cover and cook on low for 2-3 hour (the longer the better)
  9. 40 minutes prior to serving, add potatoes into the pot
  10. Add water as needed
